Inception

Director: Christopher Nolan

Genre: Science Fiction / Heist

Year Released: 2010

Awards: 4 Oscars (Cinematography, Sound Editing, Sound Mixing, Visual Effects)
Fun Fact: Filmed across six countries and deals with “dreams within dreams”.

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled “extractor” who invades the subconscious of his targets to steal secrets. The mission this time isn’t to steal an idea—but to plant one.
Cobb and his team descend into layered dream worlds and face a threat that only he can anticipate. The story explores reality vs. illusion and the cost of redemption.
